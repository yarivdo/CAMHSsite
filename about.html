About.

More About Our Team.

In order to become a team member, you need to be trained in the field of mental health and psychotherapy. For this reason we have hired a team made of nurses, social workers, psychologists and psychiatrist, all dedicated to helping you and your family.

The team is able to treat you either as an individual, or as a family. We offer a range of therpies, including cognitive/behavioural ones, talking therapies, family intervention and group therapies.

Below is a list of the current workers in CAMHS. Feel free to scroll down and track your therapist or key worker and contact them via email or via our land-line 06-7537790
